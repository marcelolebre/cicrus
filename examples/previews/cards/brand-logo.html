A 7×6 dot matrix: seven wing dots describe the ascending V, one oversized dot (scale 1.45, --warning amber) is the sun it climbs toward, and the unlit grid is held at 0.06-alpha so the mark reads as a dot-matrix display, not a drawing. The tile is panel-tier Liquid Glass: translucent fill, blur(12px) saturate(160%), the specular rim, a soft float shadow — whatever sits behind shows through it. Two inks only: white wings, amber sun; monochrome when the context wants total restraint. The mark holds to a 24px favicon; below that, drop the grid and keep the sun and wing-tips.
