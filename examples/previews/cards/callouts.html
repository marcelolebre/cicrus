Callouts
.doc-callout · --tip · --warn · --danger · --example
Note

Context the reader could miss but shouldn't. Keep it under three sentences — anything longer belongs in body prose.

Tip

A shortcut or best practice. Tips are optional reading — the document must work without them.

Warning

Something that can go wrong, with the condition stated first: when running against production, exports lock the table.

Danger

Irreversible consequences only — data loss, security exposure. If everything is danger, nothing is.

Example

A worked instance of the concept just introduced. Keep the scenario concrete and small enough to verify by hand.
