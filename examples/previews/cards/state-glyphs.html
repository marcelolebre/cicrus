THE ICARUS MARK · THE SUN CARRIES THE STATE
RUNNING
The sun breathes green and drifts gently up and down. Alive, working, in flight.
STOPPED
Sun white, perfectly still. Healthy, at rest — nothing in flight.
BROKEN
Alarm red, still. The climb has failed — pair with an alert that says why.
One per thinking object. The glyph is the app mark doubling as a status light — the wings follow the page colour, only the sun changes. Pick the smallest size that reads: 32–48px as a status header replacement, 120–180px as a stat-card hero, 200px as a dashboard tile. Running animates on transform/opacity only and honours prefers-reduced-motion; stopped and broken are static by design — a state change is an instant cut, never an ease. Broken reads in the mode's alarm red (--accent) on every surface.
