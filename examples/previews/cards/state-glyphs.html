State glyphs
96×96 PIXEL GRID · ONE THINKING-OBJECT · THREE STATES
IDLE
Wobbling putty blob. Healthy, dormant. Soap bubble in wind — never quite a sphere.
THINKING
Siri-style swirl with heartbeat ring. Active, generating, processing.
ERROR
The thinking sphere, broken. Servos failing, ring shattered. Warning red.
One per screen. The glyph is the §2.6 “moment of surprise” — having two of the same kind on one view flattens the effect. Pick the smallest size that reads: 32–48px as a status header replacement, 120–180px as a stat-card hero, 200px as a dashboard tile, 240–288px as a loading takeover. Idle and thinking inherit the canvas’s computed color; error stays warning-red on every surface.
