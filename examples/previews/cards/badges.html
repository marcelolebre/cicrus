.c-badge-accent Accent moment. Reserve for the one-and-only red item per screen.
Mixing. A task card may carry multiple badges (e.g. SESSION_RESOURCE + RECURRING) — category badge first, meta badges after. Max 3 per card.
Composition. All badges share Space Mono 10px, 0.06em letter-spacing, UPPERCASE, padding 3px 10px, border-radius 999px, 1px solid border. Color codes the label text + border; the fill is always transparent.
