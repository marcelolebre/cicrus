The wordmark is set in Doto, weight 500, letter-spacing 0.04em, lowercase letters rendered as ALL CAPS via text-transform. No tagline lockup, no logomark, no enclosing shape — the dot-matrix typography is the brand. The mark color always inherits from --text-display; in light mode it goes black, in dark it goes white. There is no colored variant, no inverse — restraint is the brand voice.
