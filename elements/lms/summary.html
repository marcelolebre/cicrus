Four moves
Lesson 03 / 09
14 min · complete
  • Rows are the system's nouns. Every row in a schematic is one kind of thing — ingest, queue, worker, store.
  • Traces are the verbs. A line between two cards encodes a real runtime flow — never a conceptual relationship, never a wish.
  • One path at a time. Pick the topmost card, follow one trace downward to the bottom, name every fork on the way.
  • The best readers see what is not drawn. A worker layer with no queue above it tells you a queue exists somewhere — just not on this canvas.
