Every operational system you will ever inherit is a wiring diagram in disguise. Learn to read the diagram and the system explains itself.

A Cicrus schematic is read top to bottom. Each row is one abstraction layer — ingest at the top, storage at the bottom, application logic in the middle.1

Press ? on any schematic page to show the legend, or L to toggle live data-flow animation on the traces. See references/schematics.md for the full primer.
